About the Author

Belle French

Twenty years old · Dreamer · Reader of many tales

My name is Belle French, and if you had told me a year ago that I would be sitting here writing on a device about love, I would have laughed and turned back to my books. Books are my world, my escape, my companions, my greatest joy. At twenty, I had read more stories than most people ever will, and yet somehow, I had never lived one of my own.

That all changed on one dark and frightening night. A night I did not choose, a path I did not plan, and a door that opened into something I never could have imagined. I stumbled — quite literally — into the life of someone the world had written off as a beast. Prince Adam was not what the whispers made him out to be. He was guarded, yes. Rough around the edges, certainly. But beneath all of that was something extraordinary, and I was just stubborn enough to stay long enough to see it.

Life since then has been nothing short of a story worth telling. The kind I used to only find between the pages of old leather-bound books. And so I decided to write it down — every strange and wonderful and unexpected piece of it — right here, for anyone who cares to read along.

If you are someone who has ever felt like they did not quite fit into the world around them, or who found something beautiful in an unlikely place, then I think you will feel right at home here. Pull up a chair, light a candle, and stay a while.

-Belle
