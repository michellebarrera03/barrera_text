About the Author

Belle French

Twenty years old · Dreamer · Reader of many tales

Hello everybody, my name is Belle French, and if you had told me a year ago that I would be sitting here writing about love, I would've laughed and turned back to my books. Books were my world, my escape, my companions haha. At 20, I had read more stories, than most people ever will, and yet somehow, I have never lived one of my own.

I decided to start this blog because so many people have questions about what really happened and what life has been since then. Here, I'll be sharing some of my favorite memories, answering questions I've been asked, and giving you a closer look at the experiences that shaped me into who I am today most of all I hope these stories help paint a better picture of my journey and the unexpected path that led me to the love of my life.

I hope you enjoy reading and maybe you'll even find a little bit of yourself in these posts too. I hope to encourage many people with my words and thoughts.

Stick along to know more about my magical in my view :)

- Belle
