About the Author

Belle French

Twenty years old · Dreamer · Reader of many tales

My name is Belle French, and if you had told me a year ago that I would be sitting here writing about love, I would have laughed and turned back to my books. Books were my world — my escape, my companions, my greatest joy. At twenty, I had read more stories than most people ever will, and yet somehow, I had never lived one of my own.

That all changed one night. It happened so fast, and before I knew it, my entire life was different.

I never expected to meet the Beast, let alone have my world turned upside down because of him. At first, I only knew the stories and rumors, but once I actually got to know him, I realized he wasn't the monster everyone believed he was. Prince Adam was kind, caring, and someone who had simply been misunderstood. Along the way, I also met so many amazing people who had been living under the same curse, and being able to help them finally find happiness is something I'll always be grateful for.

Sometimes I still can't believe this is my life. It honestly feels like I'm living in one of the fairy tales I used to read in my books back home. Since so much has happened, I thought it might be fun to share my experiences here and write about this unexpected journey.

If you've ever felt like you didn't quite fit in, or if you've ever found something wonderful where you least expected it, then I think you'll feel right at home here. Pull up a chair, light a candle, stay for a while, and maybe even chat with me.

~ Belle
