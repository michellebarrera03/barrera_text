The Night It All Happened

Written by Belle French

So many people have asked me what I felt that night, the night that everything changed for me. I feel like most people won't understand exactly what I went through especially the people who always forced me to be with the wrong person and yes, I'm talking about you Gaston but anyways back to my story.

The truth is I don't think I will ever find the perfect words for it… when I saw the last rose, petal fall, my heart sank. I thought I had lost him forever, but then something incredible happened. The beast that everyone feared and judged began to change right before my eyes- matter of fact, right before everybody's eyes!

His posture straightened, his hair turned into a golden yellow, and his face was so handsome and pretty. It wasn't just his appearance that transformed, though, it felt like his heart had returned to the person he had always been. It just needed to be awakened again.

In that moment I realized I had been right to trust him all along. People warn me to stay away… even my father, worried that I was making a terrible mistake by believing there was good hiding beneath all the anger and loneliness, but I saw something different. I saw someone who was hurting, someone capable of kindness, and someone who simply needed to be heard and understood.

As I looked around me, I took a pause to take everything in. I felt like I was looking at strangers for a moment, but then it hit me once.. Especially when Lumiere started talking, and I instantly knew who it was. The castle staff, now free from the curse! They all smiled with tears in their eyes. It felt like this whole time they had been waiting for this moment too. For the first time, I didn't feel strange for seeing the good in someone everybody else had given up on. I'm so glad I followed my heart.

More than anything I felt peace. It wasn't the kind of happiness I've read before in books. It was scary, but I knew something was calling to me. I felt Prince Adam's heart before he heard his own. I just needed him to hear it too. And once he did, it felt like peace and harmony that night taught me that love isn't about perfection or appearance, sometimes it's about believing in someone when no one else will.

And in the end, following my heart gave me that happy ending. I never thought I would have and would only read about.

- Belle

My First Impression of the BEAST

Written by Belle French

So another question I was asked was what was the first time or what was the first impression of the beast when you first met him and to be honest, I wish my reaction was a little different because at first I was so scared and mad about someone withholding my father. So of course my first emotion was anger.

But then the angered mellowed and I was left to observe what was infront of me.. So tall, such long hair or thick fur.., large claws, and sharp strong looking teeth. How can such a monster exist I thought, this is exactly what a monster would look like in the fiction books I read about.

And yet even though this scary looking monster stood infront of me I didn't even think twice about it, I told him (the monster) "Take me instead". My father means the world to me- to have him live out his days in a cold dungeon in this dark basement filled with nothing but despair and loneliness would be cruel.

And then I heard his DEEP voice- talking back to me. I was SCARED, scared to a point that my body froze and the wind from his yell hit me like a gust, his voice echoed in the room for a while too. I would be lying if I said I wasn't scared in that moment- but I persevered! And I showed him I wasn't scared.

And then once we exchange a few words, something felt different. I can't really explain it, but it almost seemed like he was hesitating when he spoke to me. Like he wasn't truly the terrifying monster he appeared to be. There was anger in his voice, yes, but there was also uncertainty, almost as if he didn't know how to talk to another person anymore.

But I remember most, though, were his eyes. His bright blue eyes. Out of everything about him, they stood out the most. I remember thinking to myself, "can someone really be a monster if their eyes look like they're filled with human emotions?" it was only a passing thought, but it stayed with me.

I won't lie though a little something inside me still felt as if something special had brewed inside my heart. Like I opened something new to my life. Looking back at it now I almost laughed at how convinced I was that I understood exactly who he was after only a few minutes.

Sometimes the people who seem the scariest are actually the ones who have been hurt the most. If I had let fear make every decision for me, I never would've discovered the gentle, caring person hidden beneath that frightening exterior.

- Belle
