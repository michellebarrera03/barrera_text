The Night It All Happened

Written by Belle French

So many people have asked me what I felt that night, the night that everything changed for me. I feel like most people won't understand exactly what I went through especially the people who always forced me to be with the wrong person and yes, I'm talking about you Gaston but anyways back to my story.

The truth is I don't think I will ever find the perfect words for it… when I saw the last rose, petal fall, my heart sank. I thought I had lost him forever, but then something incredible happened. The beast that everyone feared and judged began to change right before my eyes- matter of fact, right before everybody's eyes!

His posture straightened, his hair turned into a golden yellow, and his face was so handsome and pretty. It wasn't just his appearance that transformed, though, it felt like his heart had returned to the person he had always been. It just needed to be awakened again.

In that moment I realized I had been right to trust him all along. People warn me to stay away… even my father, worried that I was making a terrible mistake by believing there was good hiding beneath all the anger and loneliness, but I saw something different. I saw someone who was hurting, someone capable of kindness, and someone who simply needed to be heard and understood.

As I looked around me, I took a pause to take everything in. I felt like I was looking at strangers for a moment, but then it hit me once.. Especially when Lumiere started talking, and I instantly knew who it was. The castle staff, now free from the curse! They all smiled with tears in their eyes. It felt like this whole time they had been waiting for this moment too. For the first time, I didn't feel strange for seeing the good in someone everybody else had given up on. I'm so glad I followed my heart.

More than anything I felt peace. It wasn't the kind of happiness I've read before in books. It was scary, but I knew something was calling to me. I felt Prince Adam's heart before he heard his own. I just needed him to hear it too. And once he did, it felt like peace and harmony that night taught me that love isn't about perfection or appearance, sometimes it's about believing in someone when no one else will.

And in the end, following my heart gave me that happy ending. I never thought I would have and would only read about.

- Belle
